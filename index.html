NAU Yuma Baja SAE Team

This is the team representing NAU Yuma for the Baja SAE 2026 competition at Washougal, Washington.

About Us

This is the first time a team from Yuma participates in the BAJA SAE competition, and we are glad to represent this city and university.

Project Information

Baja SAE organizes a competition that challenges students to put in practice their engineering skills by designing and building an off-road vehicle that must pass through multiple tests. The goal for the challenge is to mimic a real life situation in which a group of engineerings get together to work on a project as a team, in which they will face and overcome multiple challenges in the design and manufacturing process.

Yuma Mechanical Engineering Club
(YMEC)

For easiness of working on capstone projects, the mechanical engineering students in Yuma decided to create a club. The goal for this club was not only to complete this project, but also help in future projects and provide experience and knowledge to other students. This club helped in gathering funds and building a social network with other students and sponsors.
